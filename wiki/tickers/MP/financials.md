**Valuation implication**: If 2027 NdPr spot = $85/kg (historical average
2020–2024), the floor delivers a $25/kg × 6,000 MT = **~$150M annual cash
top-up**. This is a straight government-guaranteed cash-flow stream with
~10-year tenor. At a 12% discount rate, that's ~$800M in PV — embedded
strategic value not fully reflected in trailing multiples.
