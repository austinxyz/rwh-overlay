### Gross Margin Collapse (2021–2025)

| FY | Gross Margin | Commentary |
|----|:------------:|------------|
| 2021 | 31% | Stable legacy SiC business |
| 2022 | 36% | Peak — pre-Mohawk ramp |

| 2025 | **–16%** | **Cost > Revenue per unit** — unsustainable |

**This is the central problem**. The restructuring did NOT address unit
economics. Mohawk Valley must hit yield targets for GM to recover.
